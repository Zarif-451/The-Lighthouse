About Lighthouse

Lighthouse is a personal wellbeing and self-reflection platform designed to help individuals better understand their daily habits, routines, and overall wellness.

Through thoughtful check-ins, behavioral insights, and guided reflections, Lighthouse encourages self-awareness and supports healthier lifestyle choices.

Rather than relying on intrusive questionnaires, Lighthouse gathers insights through everyday activities, reflections, and engagement patterns to provide a comfortable and user-friendly experience.
